CircSrc_SWE.m
=============================
Calculate the sound pressure generated by a circular radiator using the spherical wave expansion (SWE) method.

The sound pressure is expressed as

.. math::
   p_{n}^m(\mathbf{r})
   =
   4\pi
   p_0 
   \sum_{\ell=0}^\infty
   \mathcal{R}_{2\ell+|m|}(r)
   Y_{2\ell+|m|}^m(\hat{\mathbf{r}})
   Y_{2\ell+|m|}^m(\pi/2,0)
